Who We Are

SMSAK (Social Media Swiss Army Knife) is a desktop application developed and sold by IcecoldAI, an independent software developer based in Brussels, Belgium. This website is operated at swissarmy.app.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it's handled — in plain language.

02

Data We Collect

We collect only what we actually need:

  • Email address — if you voluntarily submit it via the newsletter/waitlist form on this website.
  • Purchase information — handled entirely by Gumroad (see Third-Party Services). We receive only order confirmation notifications; we do not store your payment details.
  • Basic analytics — if analytics are enabled, we may collect anonymised page-view data (no personal identifiers).
The SMSAK desktop app itself collects no data. It runs entirely on your local machine. Your API keys, content, and social media credentials never leave your device or pass through our servers.
03

How We Use Your Data

If you submit your email address, we use it solely to:

  • Send product updates, version release notes, and occasional tips for SMSAK users.
  • Notify you of major announcements (new tools, pricing changes, etc.).

We will never sell, rent, or share your email with any third party for marketing purposes. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

04

Third-Party Services

This website and product interact with the following third-party services:

  • Gumroad — our payment and product delivery platform. Purchases are subject to Gumroad's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree — processes email form submissions on this website. Submitted email addresses are stored by Formspree per their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's CDN, which may log your IP address per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI / xAI (Grok) — certain SMSAK tools use these APIs. Your API key and prompts are sent directly from your device to OpenAI/xAI. IcecoldAI has no visibility into this data.
05

Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) for:

  • Remembering your light/dark theme preference.
  • Storing the countdown timer start date for the discount offer.

No tracking pixels, session identifiers, or cross-site cookies are set. If analytics are added in future, this policy will be updated before they are deployed.

If you are in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your email address from our list at any time.
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to object — opt out of any processing at any time.
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Legal basis for processing (GDPR Art. 6) Email addresses collected via the newsletter form are processed on the basis of your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a)). You may withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing or emailing us.
